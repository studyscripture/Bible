Which Day Is the Sabbath?विश्राम का दिन कौन सा है?

⚡ Internal Contradiction Foundational Doctrinal Conflict

The Old Testament establishes the seventh day (Saturday) as the Sabbath, commanded by God from creation. Paul in the New Testament declares all days equal and the Sabbath law abolished in Christ. Christians split entirely on this — Saturday Adventists vs Sunday worshippers — both using the Bible.

How Old Was Jesus When He Began Ministry?यीशु ने किस उम्र में सेवकाई शुरू की?

⚡ Internal Contradiction Chronological Impossibility

Luke says Jesus was "about thirty" when he began his ministry. John's Gospel, however, records the Jews telling Jesus he is "not yet fifty" — during a conversation about his age that would be strange if he were about 33. When combined with the birth contradictions (Herod died 4 BCE, Quirinius census 6 CE), no consistent age for Jesus can be calculated from the Gospel data.
